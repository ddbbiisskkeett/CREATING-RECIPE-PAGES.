Grama's Lemon Meringue Pie


One 9-inch pie
30 min - prep time
10 min - cook time
40 min - Total
8 Servings total


INGRIDIENTS

  • -marker 1 cup white sugar
  • -marker 2 tbsp all-pupose flour
  • -marker 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • -marker 1/4 tsp salt
  • -marker 11/2 cups of water
  • -marker 2 Lemons ,juiced and zested
  • -marker 2 tbsp butter
  • -marker 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • -marker 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • -marker 4 egg whites
  • -marker 6 tbsp white sugar

  1. Preheat oven:
    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees c).
  2. Make lemon filling:

    4. Stir in butter.
